S.BHAGAT SINGH

Birth and childhood

Bhagat Singh (27 september 1907 - 23 march 1931) , was born in lyallpur district of punjab which is presently in pakistan , his father and his uncle were part of the progressive politics

Education Qualifications

completed his schooling from the village banga for few years and then he was enrolled in the Dayanand Anglo-vedic School in Lahore

IMPORTANT DATES AND HIGHLIGHTS

1923 joined national congress
1926 bombarded in lahore
1927 arrested for bombing
24 march 1931 executed to death

MAJOR CONTRIBUTIONS

  1. founded the naujawan sabha
  2. made contacts with workers and peasants party
  3. established hindustan socialist republic assosciation (HSRA)

EXECUTION

Bhagat singh , Rajgur and Sukhdev, were sentenced to death in lahore conspiracy case and were then ordered to be hanged on 24 march 1931 at 7:30 pm

OVERVIEW

born 27 september , lyallpur district of western punjab
parents sardar kishan singh , vidyaavati
spouse durga devi
assosciated organisations HSRA , naujawan bharat sabha
revolutionary activities saunders murder case on 17 december 1928
assembly bomb case , 8 april 1929
died 23 march 1931 , lahore jail
